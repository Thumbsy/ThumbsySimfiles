The full lyrics have been romanized and translated by Cardioid, massive props to him!



高鳴る鼓動は、誰も止められない
Takanaru kodou wa, dare mo tomerarenai
Nobody can stop the throbbing beat.

激しく流れる、風に身を預けて
Hageshiku nagareru, kaze ni mi o azukete
Flowing with violence, entrusting body into the wind.

音を振り切って、光より速く
oto o furikitte, hikari yori hayaku
Shaking free from sound, faster than light.

悩みや不安は、全て置き去りにして
Nayami ya fuan wa, subete okizari ni shite
Worry and anxiety are completely left behind.



無限に広がる、大空の先の
Mugen ni hirogaru, oozora no saki no.
Infinitely spread, the other side of the heavens.

いつか夢見てた、地平線の向こうへ
Itsuka yume miteta, chiheisen no mukou e.
Ever seen in a dream, to beyond the horizon.

もう振り返らない。前に突き進むんだ
mou furikaeranai. mae ni tsukisusumunda
Already not being able to look back. I plunge forward.

歪んだ世界にさよなら
Yuganda sekai ni sayonara
Bye bye distorted world.


この大空に羽ばたく鳥たちと
kono oozora ni habataku toritachi to
In this sky, together with the birds who are flapping their wings,

共に風を受け旅立つ
tomo ni kaze o uketabidatsu
I travel by the wind.

君も楽園を目指して
kimi mo rakuen o mezashite
You should also aim for paradise.

さぁ、翼を広げよう
saa, tsubasa o hirogeyou
Come now, let's spread your wings!


この世界の果てへの道しるべ
kono sekai no hate e no michishirube
The guidepost to the border of this world,

数多の星を数えて
amata no hosi o kazoete
counting many stars.

夢の終わりの時を待つ
yume no owari no toki o matsu
Waiting for the end of this dream.

今、昇る暁のもとへ
ima, noboru akatsuki no moto e
Now, to the source of the ascending daybreak.


この翼が燃え尽きても
kono tsubasa ga moetsukitemo
Even when my wings will burn out,

飛び続けて飛び続けて
tobitsutzukete tobitsutzukete
I will keep on flying, keep on flying.

この世界の果て目指して
kono sekai no hate mezashite
Reaching for the border of this world,

飛び続けて飛び続けて
tobitsutzukete tobitsutzukete
I will keep on flying, keep on flying.
